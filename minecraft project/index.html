Introduction:

For our project, we decided that we wanted to try our best to recreate the wildly popular hit game Minecraft, a sandbox-type first person survival game with many features. With Minecraft being such a complex game, we chose a couple fundamental qualities such as breaking blocks, placing blocks, a hotbar inventory, tree and mob generation, health and hunger bar, and the ability to attack mobs.

How to Play:

To interact with a block, make sure that the cursor is hovered over a block and it has the outline overlay on the block. Then, you have two choices:
• Break the block (press ‘x’ until the block breaks)
• Place a block (left click with the correct block in hand)
You can swap between different blocks in your hotbar using the keys 1-9.

Enjoy the game!
